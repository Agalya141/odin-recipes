Pasta

This is a simple pasta recipe.

Description

Pasta is a type of food made from unleavened dough, typically shaped into long, thin strips.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the sauce in a pan.
  3. Add the garlic and onion to the pan and cook until fragrant.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce mixture.
