Lasagna

This is a simple lasagna recipe.

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly the most popular in the world. It is a flat sheet of pasta, usually made from durum wheat flour and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
